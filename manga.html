Manga and Anime Video Series

The NFT Comic Book Manga Series and the Anime Video Series

Anime and Manga are both amazingly popular. In fact, between October 2019 and September 2020, a whopping 100 million households watched Anime in Neftlix alone*! If we multiply this by 2, as most countries have an average household size of 2 to 2.5 people per household, we get an amazing viewer count of 200 million people in one year via Netflix alone. This is what our Anime series aims to tap into.

As for Manga, its popularity was shown in 2020 in North America, when Maga sales went off the charts, totalling an astounding 250 million USD in that year and part of the world alone(**). It’s clear that there’s a lot of potential in making a Manga series, which is why we aim to tap into both this and the amazingly popular NFT boom as well.

Our Anime series will be handled by professional screenplay writers working together with a high quality production company. We will also have famous Anime Voice Over actors for the series, ensuring its popularity. The comic book NFT series will be a limited series that investors can buy and sell via Opensea, which we chose due to its astounding popularity. Infact, Opensea has achieved transaction volumes of 2-3.5 million USD per month (***). The screenplay writers who will be working on our Anime series will also work on the NFT Manga series to ensure consistency and high quality. Each comic book NFT will be entirely original and unique. Our Manga series will detail all of the exciting adventures our protagonists go through, starting with Kinu being kidnapped.

Our NFTs will also provide secrets on how to conquer the game; how to overtake the DireWolves; and how to earn more points to increase in-game earnings, making them an integral part of our P2E game.
