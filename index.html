OVERVIEW

The story of kinu is a role-playing game with three Shibas on a mission to rescue their abducted Shiba Puppy from the hand of a demon wolf.

Through the parallels of the blockchain comes the next level of P2E gaming where players will battle weekly to earn top score on the leaderboard.

KINU uses the latest blockchain technology to create anall-new experience, combining P2E gaming, Anime, Manga and NFTs into something entirely unique: The KinuVerse

the gameplay

BASED ON THE STORY OF KINU

The story of $KINU takes place in ancient world Japan and is a concept based around a popular anime show.

CHOOSE A CHARACTER TO PLAY

Players will be able to choose to either be Hīrō or Joō on the quest of fighting off wolves in search of their son

EARN WEEKLY PRIZES

Weekly prizes will be given to the top three players, based on their scores and position in the leaderboard.
